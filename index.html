Southern recipes

This is my personal favorite, its a little strange but you will see why after you taste it!

Here are the ingrediants you will need:

  • Cucumber
  • potatos
  • Cream
  • salt&pepper

now that we have everything lets put them together!

  1. Lets cook them potatos
  2. Slice and peel thin cucumber slices
  3. Mash and season potatos
  4. ad a knob of butter and cream into cucumber mixture
  5. Add cucumber on top of steaming potatos
